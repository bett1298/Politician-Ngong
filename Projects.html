Ongoing Projects

Ngong Water Project Launch

In a separate but equally significant event, Ngong also celebrated the launch of a water supply project, aimed at providing clean and accessible water to communities facing water shortages. The project involved the construction of boreholes, water tanks, and distribution pipelines to serve households, institutions, and public spaces. Residents expressed relief and joy, as the new water infrastructure will reduce the burden of fetching water from distant sources and help prevent waterborne diseases. Leaders emphasized the importance of the project in improving hygiene, supporting agriculture, and enhancing overall well-being in the region.

Last mile connectivity

Ngong recently witnessed the launch of the Last Mile Connectivity Project, a key government initiative aimed at ensuring every Kenyan household has access to electricity. The project extends electricity lines to remote and underserved areas within Ngong and its surroundings, connecting homes, schools, and small businesses that had remained off the grid. This development is expected to open up new opportunities for economic growth, improve education through lighting for evening studies, and enhance public safety. Local leaders and residents welcomed the initiative, noting it as a step towards bridging the rural-urban development gap.
